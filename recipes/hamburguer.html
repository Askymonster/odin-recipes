Hamburguer Recipe

Back to Home

Photo of a hamburguer with lettuce, tomato, cheese, and bacon

The hamburguer is a popular dish that consists of a ground meat patty, usually made of beef, served inside a sliced bread bun. It is often garnished with lettuce

Ingredients

  • 500g of ground beef
  • 1 onion
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • 4 slices of cheese
  • 4 slices of bacon
  • 4 hamburger buns
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. In a bowl, mix the ground beef with the finely chopped onion and garlic. Season with salt and pepper.
  2. Divide the mixture into 4 equal portions and shape them into patties.
  3. In a pan, cook the bacon until it is crispy. Remove and set aside.
  4. In the same pan, cook the hamburger patties for about 4-5 minutes on each side, or until they are cooked to your desired level of doneness.
  5. During the last minute of cooking, place a slice of cheese on top of each patty to melt.
